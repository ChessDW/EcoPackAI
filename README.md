## About the Project

Every year, over **300 million tons** of plastic packaging are produced worldwide — and most of it is never recycled. The core problem is a lack of accessible information: consumers face confusing labels and widespread greenwashing by corporations, with no easy way to compare the ecological impact of products in real time.

EcoPackAI bridges this gap by putting the power of environmental data directly in the hands of the consumer at the moment of purchase.

## Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| 📷 **Barcode Scanner** | Point your camera at any product barcode for instant detection |
| 🏆 **Eco Score (1–10)** | A clear sustainability rating — 10 means fully eco-friendly, 1 means highly harmful |
| 🟢🟡🔴 **Color Rating** | Visual system: green (good), yellow (moderate), red (harmful) |
| 🌿 **Green Alternatives** | Suggests eco-friendly substitutes with lower packaging impact |
| 📊 **Carbon Tracker** | Personal dashboard tracking your cumulative environmental footprint |
| 📚 **Educational Content** | Tips on materials, recycling habits, and how to reduce your impact |

---

## How It Works

```
1. SCAN   →  Open the app and scan any product barcode with your phone camera
2. ANALYZE →  AI cross-references the packaging data against the cloud green database
3. SCORE  →  Receive an Eco Score + color rating in under 5 seconds
4. CHOOSE →  Browse greener alternatives with better packaging scores
```

---

## Technology Stack

- **Computer Vision** — Detects barcodes through the phone camera in under 1 second
- **AI / Machine Learning** — Model trained on thousands of packaging materials to classify environmental impact
- **Cloud Green Database** — Synchronized product data storage, always up to date
- **Data Analytics** — Tracks shopping history and calculates long-term sustainability metrics
- **API Integration** — Connected to certified environmental databases (U.S. EPA, Green Seal)
- **Cross-Platform** — Available on iOS and Android, completely free to download

## Environmental Impact

EcoPackAI addresses the plastic packaging crisis through six key impact areas:

1. **Less Plastic** — Informed consumers reduce demand for harmful packaging
2. **Consumer Awareness** — Real-time education at the point of purchase
3. **Corporate Pressure** — Market signals push brands toward greener packaging
4. **Lower CO₂ Emissions** — Less plastic production = less industrial energy = fewer greenhouse gases
5. **Better Recycling** — Teaches users how to separate and recycle materials correctly
6. **Environmental Literacy** — Every scan is a lesson in sustainability
