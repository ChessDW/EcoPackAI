How it works

Four steps to a
greener choice.

The whole process takes less than five seconds. All you need is your smartphone.

01

Scan the Barcode

Open the app and point your camera at any product's barcode. Our computer vision detects it instantly.

02

AI Analyzes the Packaging

Our machine learning model cross-references the product against our cloud-based green database, trained on thousands of packaging materials.

03

Get Your Eco Score

Receive a score from 1 to 10 and a color rating — green means great, yellow means moderate, red means harmful.

⚡ Results in under 5 seconds
04

Discover Green Alternatives

If the score is low, EcoPackAI suggests eco-friendly substitutes with better packaging that are available nearby or online.
